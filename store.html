In the mean time, I shall recommend some video game to you, all of which can be bought of of steam(I never said this would only be MY store). First off, My Summer Car. You play as a late teen in the Finnish summer fixing up an old car. The game is fairly difficult, but I think it is fun, regardless if the page says it is not a fun game. Second, Lucid blocks. On the surface, it looks like a Minecraft clone, but the only real similarity is the voxel based nature of the game. It diverts into a unique crafting system where any item works for a recipe, it features surreal graphics that run on potato computers(ie, this i7 6500u laptop), and it's only $9.99. Finally, elite dangerous. It does require a bit more of an advanced computer(my laptop struggles to run it on Window 10/11, and fails to continue past 5 minutes on Arch.), but even a fx 8350, 16gb ddr3, and a gtx 1070 can work. You play as a commander in a post FTL travel galaxy and can basically do anything. It goes on sale a lot.
