Introduction

I am Lead Lerner, and this is my website. I like building games and stuff. Currently I am building a 2d rpg in c# and monogame. These projects will likely be abandoned and switched out for whatever catches my eye at the current moment. I just recently got to the point where I can separate my test game from the engine, instead of the game itself being the engine. I am also working on a operating system, fittingly named leadOS, using c++ and assembly. Anyway, this website is basically going to be where I post my games, projects, and just random thoughts I happen to have.

What to expect

You should expect this website to receive updates at an regular interval. You should not expect me to delve into politics either. This site is mainly geared towards ametuer computer programmers. Half the time I don't know what I am talking about, so if you don't understand what I am saying, chances are I don't either. I will be posting more images to thi site, and will likely change the style drastically very so often.
